Cuties In Need of Forever Homes

Goldy the Goldfish

Goldy will do best in a home where they are the only fish. They enjoy snacks and will do tricks for you when you feed them. Goldy also really loves a castle in a tank if you want to please them.

Banty the Bantam Rooster

Banty is generally a good boy but he is a rooster's rooster. He needs a forever home either far away from neighbors or somewhere where everyone likes to get up at the crack of dawn.

Snootie the Teacup Pig

Snootie is "Some Pig." Snootie has been through some trauma as her previous owner with whom she bonded recently passed away. With a little patience Snootie can bond with you and be your new best friend.
